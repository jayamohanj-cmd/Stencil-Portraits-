How to use the templates

  1. Cut. Print each layer and cut out the black areas with a craft knife — or send the SVG files to a laser cutter or Cricut. The black areas are the openings the paint passes through.
  2. Silhouette pass. Tape layer 1 to your canvas and spray the lightest colour. Lift carefully.
  3. Mid-tone pass. Line up layer 2 on the corners and spray the darker colour. Wait ~10 minutes between coats.
  4. Detail pass. Line up layer 3 and spray the darkest colour for the fine details. Reveal your portrait!
