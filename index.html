How to use the templates

  1. Cut. Print each layer and cut out the black areas with a craft knife — or send the SVG files to a laser cutter or Cricut. The black areas are the openings the paint passes through.
  2. Base pass. Tape layer 1 to your canvas and spray the lightest colour. Lift carefully.
  3. Build up. Align each next layer on the ✚ corner marks and spray its colour, light to dark. Wait ~10 minutes between coats.
  4. Detail pass. The last layer is the darkest — spray, lift, and reveal your portrait!
